Aloo Poori

Aloo Poori is a traditional Indian dish that consists of a spiced Potato Curry (Aloo Sabzi) served with Puri, an Indian fried bread. This hearty and satisfying meal is a favorite weekend treat in many Indian households

The Aloo Sabzi is made with ingredients like potatoes, onions, ginger, garlic, green chillies, tomatoes, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, garam masala, asafoetida, and dry mango powder.The Puri is made from whole wheat flour, and it’s deep-fried until it puffs up1.

Ingredients

  • 3 medium-sized potatoes
  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 2 tablespoons semolina
  • ¼ teaspoon turmeric powder
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon crushed kasuri methi
  • Water
  • Oil

Steps

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the mashed potatoes, whole wheat flour, semolina, turmeric powder, chili powder, cumin powder, garam masala, carom seeds, crushed kasuri methi, and salt
  2. Mix everything well to form a crumbly mixture
  3. Gradually add water to the potato mixture and knead it into a smooth and firm dough. Adjust the water as needed
  4. The dough should be pliable and not too sticky. Cover it with a damp cloth and let it rest for 15-20 minutes
  5. Shape the Pooris
  6. Deep Fry the Pooris
